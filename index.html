How it works

A quick walkthrough of the basics — start a shift, log tasks on your counters, and review your history later.

Cards, taps, and a running tally

Each counter is its own card — tap + to log a task, − to undo a slip of the finger. The dashed card is the "＋" tile for adding a new counter (up to 8). Your total and per-counter progress update instantly at the top of the page.

1

Set your name

The first time you open the tracker, pick your name from the list (or add your own). It's shown in the badge up top and can be changed there any time.

2

Start a shift

Tap "Start Shift" to begin tracking — you'll be asked for your name first if you haven't set one. Everything you log gets grouped under that shift, tagged with your name, until you end it.

3

Log tasks

Tap + on a counter each time you finish one. Use − or the Undo button if you tap by mistake. Keys 1–8 log by card position too.

4

Take a break

Tap Break to pause — logging is disabled until you resume. Break time is saved with the shift and editable later.

5

Customize counters

Tap the gear on a card to rename it, set a color and a daily/weekly/monthly goal. Hold ✕ to remove a counter, or use ⧉ to duplicate one.

6

Reorder counters

Drag a card by its ⠿⠿ handle onto another to swap their positions — arrange them however fits your workflow.

7

End & review

Tap End Shift (twice, to confirm) to save it to history. Click any row to edit counts, times, or break duration, filter the table by name, or export a date range to CSV.

Where is my data stored?

Everything is saved in this browser's local storage on this device — nothing is sent anywhere. It stays put across closes and restarts, but won't follow you to a different browser or computer, and clearing this browser's site data will erase it.

Can I fix a shift after it's ended?

Yes — click any row in Shift History to edit its date, start/end time, break duration, or the counts for each counter. You can also delete a shift entirely from there.

What happens if I remove a counter mid-shift?

The counter disappears from the grid, but any tasks already logged on it still count toward the shift's total — that work isn't lost, it just won't show as its own line anymore. Removing is undoable right after you do it, via the toast that appears.

How do goals work?

Set a daily, weekly, or monthly target on a counter (via its gear icon) and the card shows live progress toward it, resetting automatically at the start of each new period.

Do I have to pick a name?

Yes — "Start Shift" is blocked until a name is set, so every shift in the history is attributed to someone. Pick from the roster or add your own via "Other…".

What happens if I change my name mid-shift?

It only applies going forward. The shift already in progress stays logged under whoever started it — you'll see a confirmation of that when you make the change — and the new name takes effect from your next shift on.

Can I see just my own shifts?

Yes — once the history has more than one name in it, a filter dropdown appears above Shift History so you can narrow the table down to a single agent. CSV export is still date-range based and includes everyone in that range, with an Agent column in the file.
